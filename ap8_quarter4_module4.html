Modyul 4: Epekto ng Ideolohiya, Cold War, at Neokolonyalismo sa Iba't ibang Bahagi ng Daigdig

Overview

This module discusses the impact of ideology, the Cold War, and neocolonialism on different parts of the world.

  • Understand the impact of various ideologies.
  • Identify key events during the Cold War.
  • Recognize the effects of neocolonialism.
  • Analyze examples of these impacts.
